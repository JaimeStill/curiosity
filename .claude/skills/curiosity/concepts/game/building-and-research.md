# Building and research (concept)

## Question

How does building serve research-station construction without becoming the central gameplay loop — what role does the build verb play in a contemplative, exploration-forward experience, and what would make it feel less like a chore than typical voxel-survival building?

## Constraints already locked in

- Building serves research stations, which support gradually augmenting the player; the loop is not the central gameplay verb (`design/game/premise.md` — Player).
- Bases, research stations, and crafted vessels persist across sessions and accumulate as the player's footprint within a larger world that does not (`design/game/premise.md` — Aspirational targets, Persistence).
- The pace of the experience is deliberate; building should sustain that pace rather than fight it (`design/game/premise.md` — Player).
- Building interacts with vessel-embodiment (`concepts/game/vessel-embodiment.md`): crafted vessels are one of the persistent artifacts the build loop produces, and the relationship between building and vessel construction is itself an open question.
