- `experiments/ecs-storage-comparison/workload/components.go` — `Position` and `Velocity` structs (3×float32 each).
- `experiments/ecs-storage-comparison/workload/iteration.go` — `IterationSetup` (spawns N entities with deterministic Position-by-index and uniform Velocity) and `IterationTick` (queries Position+Velocity, integrates motion through unsafe pointer access).
